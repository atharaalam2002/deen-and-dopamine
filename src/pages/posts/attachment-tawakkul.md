**Reframe:** People can fail you; Allah does not. Build *secure base* with du‘a, salah on time, and consistent service to others.

**Exercises:**
- Anxious: Delay texting by 10 minutes; use that window for du‘a + grounding.
- Avoidant: One vulnerability rep per day — say one honest sentence.
- Both: *Husn al-dhann* journal: write the best possible intention for others’ actions.
